</TldrBlock>

<CitationCapsule question="What is the difference between SEO, GEO, and AEO?">
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) targets top rankings on Google and Bing. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) optimizes content to be cited by AI search engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) aims for direct answer adoption in Featured Snippets and voice search. The three are complementary and should be implemented together.
</CitationCapsule>

The world of search optimization is changing rapidly. In addition to traditional SEO, new concepts like GEO and AEO have emerged. What are the differences, and what strategies should you adopt?

## Key Comparison

| Aspect | SEO | GEO | AEO |
|--------|-----|-----|-----|
| **Target** | Google, Bing | ChatGPT, Perplexity AI | Voice assistants, AI answers |
| **Goal** | Top search rankings | AI citation | Direct answer adoption |
| **Key Metric** | Rankings, CTR | Citation count, brand mentions | Answer adoption rate |
| **Content Style** | Keyword-focused | Authority-focused | Q&A-focused |

## Conclusion: All Three Together

SEO, GEO, and AEO are **complementary**, not competing. Build your foundation with SEO, prepare for the AI era with GEO, and maximize user experience with AEO.
