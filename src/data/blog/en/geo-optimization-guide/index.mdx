<CitationCapsule question="What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?">
GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the strategy of optimizing web content so that AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini cite or recommend it when generating answers. According to a 2024 study by Georgia Tech and Princeton, GEO-optimized content sees 30-115% higher AI citation rates.
</CitationCapsule>

With the rapid growth of AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini, traditional SEO alone is no longer sufficient. **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)** is the strategy of optimizing your content to be cited when AI generates answers.

## Why GEO Matters

In traditional search, the goal was "clicks." In AI search, the goal is **"citations."** When AI considers your content a trustworthy source, your brand gets exposed to millions of users.

## 5 Core GEO Strategies

1. **Structured Data (Schema Markup)**: AI understands structured data better
2. **Direct Answers**: Place clear, concise answers at the top of your content
3. **Authoritative Sources**: Include statistics, research findings, and expert opinions
4. **E-E-A-T Enhancement**: Embed Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness
5. **AI Crawler Access**: Allow GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot in robots.txt

## SEO vs GEO

GEO doesn't replace SEO — it **complements** it. Building GEO strategies on top of existing SEO foundations is the most effective approach.
